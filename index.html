Hi there!

It’s nice to meet you! I’m Christine—a Product Designer at Yesware by day and stationery hoarder by night. I can help you create and develop comprehensive and compelling solutions that your users will enjoy.

As a User Experience Designer, it is my responsibility to then take the most provoking parts of everyone’s story and thread them together into an interactive experience that everyone can understand and enjoy.

I believe that working in a close-knit, collaborative, and multi-functional environment yields the best designs. When multiple perspectives come together, we’re able to explore the extremes and stretch our boundaries while maintaining a short feedback loop.
